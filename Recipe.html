After acquiring all the neccessary ingridients now it will be appropriate to start our cooking.

PROCEDURE

  • In a clean sufuria wash your meat that is well sliced and let it boil until the water finishes.
  • Pour in your cooking oil that if enough and let the meat fry up.
  • Cut your onions and tomatoes into sizable pieces in different dishes.
  • Place the onions on the cooking oil while you turn then and when they are brown enough place the tomtatoes too.
  • When your spices have intergrated well add your masala,ginger and corriander and stir well.
  • At this stage youu can add your cocoa nut juice and add some quontified amount of water.
  • After 15min of boiling you can add your clean rice and stir it well and let it boil until the water vapourises.
