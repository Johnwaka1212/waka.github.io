********** RESEARCH ON MAIZE PRODUCTION IN TRANS-NZOIA COUNTY **********

If you want to calculate the number of bag sizes produced per acre ,the following should be conducted.

CONSINDERATIONS
  1. quality of the seeds
  2. type
    of ploughing machines
  3. early cultivation
  4. type of soil
  5. planting seasons
  6. topography of the land
  7. pest & diseases
  8. heap of organic manure
  9. cost

After consindering all the neccessary requirements now it will be appropriate to start our preparations.

PROCEDURE
  • before the onset of rain,plough your land on time
  • after the first droplets of rain apply 1,1/2 KG of DAP fertiliser.
  • Pour in the hybrid maize seedlings on the rows & cover gentle with little soft soil.
  • wait for about one week after germination for sprouting of leaflets.
  • apply the first time weeding to when the maize is about 30cm above the ground.
  • apply some chemicals to keep away pest diseases like fall armyworm.
  • apply the second weeding to outcompete the growing weeds & also to reduce conjestion & struggle for lights
  • After 1-2 months apply 100KG of CAN ferliser to boost the growth.
  • set the maize to dry after pollinations,approximately after 3 weeks they are ready & put them on stake awaiting for harvesting.
